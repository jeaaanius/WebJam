TF Web Jam Experience

Webtite
  • May 20, 2022
  • 2 min to read

“Tara G?!” lead us here in joining this competition since it is our last year in college, and we are wondering on how can we make it more memorable and a bit challenging. Luckily, the annual TechnoFest has just started and a lot of exciting events prepared for the students of FEU Tech, FEU Alabang, and FEU Diliman. When we saw the poster for the Web Jam competition, we immediately created a group to dive in to the excitement of the TechnoFest experience. As we ideate on the WebJam, it triggers the memories that we’ve shared all throughout our college life that brought out the concept of our design – a simple yet vibrant interface that signifies how fun and exciting TechnoFest has been.

WebJam 2022 gave us the opportunity to showcase our talent and creativity. During the 4-day development of this website, we are able to enjoy the strenuous process despite the challenges of collaborating online. This proves that despite the pandemic we can still cultivate an environment where entertainment meets learning. With that, we are grateful for the organizers of TechnoFest 2022, especially the WebJam committee. See you on the awarding night!
