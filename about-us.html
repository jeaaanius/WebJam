Alabang, Diliman, and FEU Institute of Technology

The integrated schools of FEU Alabang, FEU Diliman, and FEU Institute of Technology are a vibrant community of technology scholars and professionals seeking to serve the Nation, the Industry, and the World.

Their rich, vibrant community allows students to make life-long friends, have unforgettable moments, get real-world training, and enjoy the ideal campus life.

TECHNOFEST 2022

The TECHNOFEST 2022 is a convention that celebrates the bounty of technology that this generation has to offer to its people. Esteemed guests and speakers are invited to talk for Students of FEU Tech, Diliman, and Alabang, as well as from different colleges and universities, on matters like challenges and technology fallbacks.

Delegates from other colleges and universities could also join competitions and paper presentations related to their degree and program. This program will celebrate technology by boosting its participants’ enthusiasm that shall trigger creativity and innovation that shall make a mark for centuries to come.
